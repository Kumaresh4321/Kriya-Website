    Mr. Karthik Karuppu ENERGY RESOURCES FOR GREEN BUILDING



    “The importance of renewable energy” given by Mr. Karthik Karuppu, a BEE certified energy auditor and NSC electrical safety engineer. He has done energy audits at thermal and gas power plants at various government and private projects like NTPC Faraka, Tata power corporation limited, HMPC, TNVL, HPGCL etc., He has also been involved in BEE/EESL projects like AgDSM, PAT and MuDSM. He is an eminent speaker and has expertise in energy technologies such as EV charging, solar energy, green building, energy conservation and electrical safety.
