ADVANCED DESIGN TECHNIQUES & TOOLS IN AUTODESK FUSION 360

ASME offers an opportunity for the budding engineers to explore their future work environment. This workshop introduces the participants into the world of advanced design techniques and tools in Autodesk Fusion 360. Autodesk is ushering into the new era of engineering design using its brain child Fusion 360, which is a cloud-based 3D modelling, CAD, CAM, CAE, and PCB software platform for product design and manufacturing. The notable takeaways from this workshop would be freeform modelling, creating advanced T spline models etc using Autodesk Fusion 360.Moreover, the participants can explore the ‘Generative Design’ module in Fusion 360, which is the culmination of AI and cloud computing by which the software itself would propose the best manufacturing method for the component designed and also compare the various manufacturing methods available for the given component.

INTRODUCTION TO FUSION 360

Fusion 360 is important for Mechanical eNgineers because it is a utility software tool that can do everything in one place. One can create 3D designs, collaborate, manage data, create toolpaths, run simulations to validate designs and much more. It is a next generation design tool that will enable aspiring engineers to bring to life their ideas and contribute to society. In this workshop, participants will be a part of hands-on one day course that will cover the basics of AutoDesk Fusion 360 and provide exposure to spome of its numerous capabilities.

THERMO-MECHANICAL SIMULATION FOR MANUFACTURING

Description
This workshop aims at providing introduction to thermo-mechanical simulation which is the exact reproduction of the thermal and mechanical processes in the laboratory that a material is subjected during the manufacturing process. Practical simulation will be carried out using Gleeble 3500 Thermo-Mechanical Simulator available in Centre of Excellence in Welding, PSG College of Technology.
SESSION 1
Lecture: Introduction to Thermo-Mechanical simulation. In this lecture insights of scope and application of the Thermo-Mechanical Simulation in material processes will be discussed.

SESSION 2
Practical: Simulation trial demonstration using Gleeble 3500. Working of Gleeble 3500, demonstration of Hot Tensile Test, Hot Compression Test and HAZ simulation will be carried out.
